About the Workshop

Cybersecurity 101

This workshop will help you understand the basics of cybersecurity and how to protect your data and systems from various digital threats. Through hands-on activities and expert instruction, you will learn how to recognize and mitigate cybersecurity risks in today's world.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Introduction to cybersecurity and its importance
  • Common types of cyber threats: malware, phishing, and social engineering
  • Understanding firewalls, encryption, and secure passwords
  • Protecting personal and professional data
  • Best practices for safe online behavior

Who Should Attend?

  • Beginners interested in cybersecurity
  • Students and professionals looking to improve their digital security knowledge
  • Anyone who uses online services and wants to stay secure
